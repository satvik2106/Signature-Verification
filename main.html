Ensuring Every Signature is Genuine

Visit the SBI website Click here

Forging a signature is considered a form of fraud. It involves deceiving

others by falsely representing someone's authorization or consent,

which is illegal and can result in various legal consequences, including

fines and imprisonment.

Signature-based detection is one of the most direct and well-established

methods to identify malicious activity. Signature-based detection examines

network traffic, compares it to known signatures, and generates an alert

when a match is made.
